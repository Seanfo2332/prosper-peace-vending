Who We Are
Your Vending Machine Solution

Established since 1998, PROSPER PEACE VENDING SDN. BHD. is a leading vending machine, products, and system solutions provider with special emphasis on meeting customers' refreshment needs. Our headquarter is situated in Penang, Malaysia. We have the largest geographical footprint as an independent vending solutions provider within the Northern Region of Peninsular Malaysia.

With more than 20 years of experience in the vending machine, we have been growing from strength to strength and gaining reputation amongst our business partners and peer competitors. With our strong mission to provide more comprehensive sets of services on retail convenience, we set our sight on becoming one of the market leaders in the automation technology industry.

Company: PROSPER PEACE VENDING SDN. BHD.

Registration: SSM: 202201039206 / 1484903-U

Headquarters: Bukit Mertajam, Penang, Malaysia

Our company is also a cutting-edge company at the forefront of revolutionizing payment systems with its specialization in cashless transactions. With a commitment to seamless and secure financial interactions, we have developed state-of-the-art cashless payment systems that cater to the evolving needs of businesses and consumers alike. One of our flagship products is the Ewallet QR System, a user-friendly and efficient solution that enables individuals to make transactions effortlessly through a simple scan of a QR code.

Our Journey

Company Milestones

A timeline of growth, innovation, and commitment to excellence over more than two decades.

1998

Company Founded

Prosper Peace Vending was established in Bukit Mertajam, Penang, starting as a local vending machine operator serving the community.

2005

Multi-State Expansion

Expanded operations beyond Penang to serve multiple states across Peninsular Malaysia, establishing regional partnerships.

2010

100+ Machines Deployed

Reached the milestone of deploying over 100 vending machines across various locations including offices, factories, and public spaces.

2015

Cashless Payment Integration

Pioneered cashless payment integration in our vending machines, embracing Malaysia's shift towards digital transactions.

2020

Ewallet QR Technology

Adopted Ewallet QR payment technology supporting Touch 'n Go, Boost, GrabPay, ShopeePay, and other major Malaysian e-wallets.

2022

Restructured as Sdn Bhd

Company restructured and officially registered as Prosper Peace Vending Sdn. Bhd. (SSM: 202201039206 / 1484903-U), marking a new chapter of corporate growth.

2024

500+ Machines Nationwide

Achieved the landmark of 500+ vending machines deployed across all 13 states of Malaysia, solidifying our position as a nationwide leader.
