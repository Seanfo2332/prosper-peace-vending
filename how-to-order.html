How to Order Your Vending Machine

From first inquiry to full installation — here's how the process works.

Step by Step

Your Journey to Vending Success

We've made the ordering process simple and straightforward. Here's how it works:

1

Contact & Consultation

Reach out to us via WhatsApp, phone, or our contact form. Our team will understand your needs, location, and budget to recommend the best vending solution.

2

Location Assessment

We'll assess your proposed location to ensure optimal placement, power supply availability, and foot traffic for maximum profitability.

3

Quotation & Agreement

Receive a detailed quotation with pricing, machine specifications, and service terms. Once agreed, we'll proceed with your order.

4

Payment & Confirmation

Make your payment via bank transfer, online banking, or e-wallet. We accept flexible payment options to suit your convenience.

5

Delivery & Installation

Our team will deliver and install your vending machine at your location. We handle setup, testing, and configuration of all payment systems.

6

Training & After-Sales Support

We provide full training on machine operation, restocking, and troubleshooting. Plus 24/7 technical support and warranty coverage.
